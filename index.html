About Us

Evara Interiors is a bespoke Interior Design Studio based out of Chennai, creating homes that are timeless, functional, and uniquely personal. We design spaces that reflect your lifestyle, aspirations, and daily rituals.

With a thoughtful, end-to-end approach, we manage every stage of the journey — from Planning and Design to Seamless Execution and Styling.

Founded by Ar. Priyanka Nagarajan, Evara Interiors has designed 100+ homes across Tamil Nadu, each shaped by the belief that "Every space has a story; our role is to help it unfold."
