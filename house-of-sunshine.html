RESIDENTIAL · CHENNAI · 2024 · 1300 SQ.FT

House of Sunshine

The House of Sunshine is a 1300 sq.ft residential interior in Chennai, designed around the concept of warmth, elegance, and intentional living. After thoughtful analysis of the client's lifestyle, the design intent was simple — elegant, cozy, and filled with thoughtful light layering. Warm tones, carefully curated furniture, and layered lighting transform this Chennai home into a personal sanctuary. Every corner reflects the family's personality, making the space feel authentically theirs. A home that doesn't just look beautiful — but feels like coming home.

Living Room

The living room is anchored by a bold mustard sofa against warm neutrals — elegant, cozy, and full of personality.

Master Bedroom

A marble-accent feature wall with layered lighting creates a luxurious yet restful retreat.

Kitchen

A U-shaped kitchen in white and gold — maximising space while maintaining a premium finish.
