Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026  ·  Evara Interiors, Chennai

Evara Interiors ("we", "us", "our"), operated by Ar. Priyanka Nagarajan, is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website (evarainteriors.in) or contact us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, phone number, email address, and city — when you submit our contact form
  • Project details and messages you share with us
  • Any information you voluntarily share via WhatsApp, phone, or email

We also collect non-personal information automatically via Google Analytics (see Section 4).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiry and provide design consultation
  • To communicate about your project, appointments, and proposals
  • To send you relevant updates about Evara Interiors (you may opt out at any time)
  • To improve our website and services based on usage patterns

3. Contact Form & Data Storage

Our contact form is powered by Formspree, a third-party service. When you submit the form, your data is transmitted securely to Formspree and forwarded to our email. Formspree's privacy policy applies to data processed through their platform. We do not store your form submissions on our own servers.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our website. This service collects anonymised data such as pages visited, time spent, device type, and general location (city level). No personally identifiable information is collected by Google Analytics. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. WhatsApp

Our website includes a WhatsApp button for direct communication. When you use it, your conversation is subject to WhatsApp's (Meta's) own privacy policy. We do not record or store WhatsApp conversations outside of the WhatsApp platform.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for marketing purposes. We only share data with service providers (Formspree, Google Analytics) as described above, and only to the extent necessary to operate our website and services.

7. Data Security

Our website is served over HTTPS (SSL encryption). We take reasonable precautions to protect your information, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of any marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at evarainteriors@gmail.com.

9. Cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymised usage data. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies for analytics purposes. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be published on this page with the updated date.
